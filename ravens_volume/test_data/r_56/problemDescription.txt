Yearly numbers of sunspots from 1749 to present, as from the World Data Center, aka SIDC. 
This is the version of the data that will occasionally be updated when new counts become available.
The univariate time series sunspot.year contains 289 observations, 60% of which has been used for trainData and 
the rest for testData. The objective is to predict the yearly sunspots for the years included in the testData.
